The Best Movies According to Ken

My top 3 movies of all time.

Star Wars

I saw this movie when I was 14 years old and it really had an impact on me. My interests changed immediately!

Blade Runner

This was another movie that had a profound impact on me when I was young. I even started dressing in a similar manner to the lead character (my best friend did too!)

Captain America: The First Avenger

This is probably my favorite of the "super hero movies". It didn't have the same impact as my first two selections (probably because I saw it when I was older), but it is still one of my favorite movies.
